📊 Annual Reserve Contributions: Current vs. Recommended

⚠️ The Gap: The HOA currently contributes $55,022/year to reserves. The Reserve Study recommends $86,200 in 2026 alone — a shortfall of $31,178. By 2030, the recommended contribution more than triples to $211,000, while the current contribution remains flat. The board is not funding reserves at the level their own experts recommend.

📉 Reserve Fund Health: Percent Funded Over Time

📋 Context: Even if the HOA follows the study's recommended contributions (the increasing amounts shown above), the fund still drops from 56.7% funded to 9.3% funded by 2035. Industry standard for a healthy reserve is 70%+. Below 40% is considered critically underfunded. This means the suggested contributions are the minimum — and the current contributions fall far below even that.
